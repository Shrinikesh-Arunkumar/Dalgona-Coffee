Dalgona Coffee Recipe

By Chef Shrinikesh

This yummy South Korean coffee will make your mouth water. It only requires four ingredients that you can find in your home -you feel like you won a Jackpot, right

ingredients

  • Instant Coffee or Expresso Granules
  • Hot Water
  • Suger
  • Milk

Dalgona Making Process

step 1: combine the suger, hot water and instant coffee
Add two tablespoons of instant coffee,two tbsp of hot water.
step 1:mix until smooth and fluffy
Using a electric hand mixer.
Use a spoon to garnish a glassof hot or cold milk with the coffee cream Serve with straw, stir, stir, stir to enjoy
